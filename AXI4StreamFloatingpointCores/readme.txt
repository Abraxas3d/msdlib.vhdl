IEEE compliant AXI4-Stream wrappers for FloPoCo floating point cores

developed by Omar Khaliq 2013

The provided wrappers add IEEE compliancy and AXI4-Stream handshaking to the cores available in the FloPoCo generator library.
They may be used for ASIC synthesis.



------------------------------------------------------------------

Version 1 deal with Area saving 

------------------------------------------------------------------

Version 2  deal with high throughput rate

------------------------------------------------------------------

Version 3 deal with Normalized and IEEE special numbers only

------------------------------------------------------------------

Synthesis reports are present in the seperate folder
